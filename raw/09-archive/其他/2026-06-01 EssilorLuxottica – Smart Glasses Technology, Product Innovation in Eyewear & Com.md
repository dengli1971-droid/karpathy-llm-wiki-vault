# EssilorLuxottica – Smart Glasses Technology, Product Innovation in Eyewear & Competitive Dynamics

**日期**: 2026-06-01 | **来源**: forum.thirdbridge.com | **公司**: Apple, Google, Meta, Samsung | **标签**:  | **Ticker**: 005930 KS, AAPL US, BC94 LN, GOOGL US, META US

## 核心观点
- EssilorLuxottica's 15,000 retail stores represent structural moat that competitors such as Google, Warby Parker and Samsung cannot easily replicate, given majority of smart glasses are currently sold through physical retail
- C80% of EssilorLuxottica's smart business is in entry-level products, primarily in USD 299-350 retail price range. Prescription buyers currently represent a small fraction of smart glasses sales. Expert believes prescription-based sales reaching 30-40% of total smart glasses revenue by year-end 2027 would be a significant strategic success
- Smart glasses margins align closer to consumer electronics than traditional eyewear, making category currently margin-dilutive for EssilorLuxottica, despite strong top-line growth contributions. Expert notes that subscription model for premium AI features could emerge as early as 2027 as one pathway to offset margin dilution in smart eyewear
- Medtech health metrics embedded in eyewear – including eye health monitoring and hearing enhancement – are viewed as highest-value future innovation frontier for EssilorLuxottica
